About Me

After spending time working in the finance industry I decided to take a break to become a caregiver. As I prepared to re-enter the workforce, I came up with a game plan to pursue a career in technology.

My passion for creating led me down the path of Front-End Web Development. While participating in various online training programs to learn web development, I acquired the skills to complete various front-end and full-stack projects. In doing this training, I was also led down the path of Cloud technology and Devops. Realizing that this was going to be a much needed skill going forward into my new career path, I obtained skills in all major cloud Platforms. On this journey I have built projects and obtained certifications on the AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud platforms.

I am now a certified Salesforce professional and I'm passionate about using technology to drive business growth and improve customer experiences. I recently passed the Salesforce Sales Cloud Consultant exam. I am currently participating in the Clicked Training Program getting collaboration experience with peers in Admin and Business Analyst team sprints. I recently completed the Deloitte Salesforce Consultant Quest with Clicked, gaining consulting experience on a REAL project. I will continue to enhance my Salesforce skills on Trailhead when needed, and I'm planning to do some non-profit work with Salesforce after the Consultant Quest to gain more experience before I attempt more certifications.

My passion for creativity, problem solving, and learning new tools to enhance my technical skills, is a driving factor in my life. But when the opportunity arises, I enjoy reading, spending time on the beach feeling the breeze and listening to the waves, and playing the video game Destiny 2 with family and friends.

In this project I created a Lambda that converted a caller's phone number to vanity numbers when they phoned into an Amazon Connect Contact Center. The best 5 vanity numbers along with the caller's number will be saved in a DynamoDB table. An Amazon Connect contact flow will be created that repeats the caller's phone number and vanity possibilities that the Lambda function returns.

Python, Docker, Git and GitHub, Amazon Connect, AWS Lambda, DynamoDB, SAM CLI
